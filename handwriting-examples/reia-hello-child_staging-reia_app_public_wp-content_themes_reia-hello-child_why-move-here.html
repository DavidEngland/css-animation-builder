Why Move Here

Huntsville is ranked the #1 metropolitan area in the U.S. for 2022-23 by U.S. News & World Report!

This recognition is based on a variety of factors, including housing affordability, quality of life, desirability, resident well-being, job market, air quality, commute time, crime rate, and access to quality health care.

Beyond the rankings, Huntsville and its surrounding communities offer incredible opportunities to explore the outdoors, enjoy diverse cuisine, shop, experience vibrant nightlife, and immerse yourself in rich cultural events.
